Zoomies

Convenience


Zoomies take convinence and freedom up to the next level. With zoomies, your hands will be free to do whatever you want. We promise that your arms will never feel sore again. The small footprint allows it to be easily slid into a pocket or purse.

Made in USA


Zoomies durable plastic and high-quality lenses are made 100% in the United States. By buying our product, you are supporting the hard working citizens of the United States.

High-Tech Features


The high tech hardware of the zoomies provides many perks. Zoomies are zoomable to 300%. Zoomies are also equipped with other high-tech features such as a focus adjuster and built-in sunshades.

Superdad

As an avid hunter and having the innate ability to sniff out a great deal, I purchased the zoomies to take my Midwest hunting game to a new level. I was able to see Whitetail deer as far away as 1000 yards with enough Clarity to ensure that I was not in fact shooting an antlered doe! I can now easily discern A hooded Merganser and A teal from all the way across an icy swamp. My only word of caution would be to not forget that you were wearing them. There I was in the early Springwoods, hunting the elusive eastern gobbler. I was sitting at the base of a Shagbark Hickory and listening to the chortle and drumming of several adult gobblers and, passed into a slumber. I woke up and suddenly realize there was a 47 pound loggerhead snapping turtle mere centimeters in front of my feet. I cautiously moved my hand to my Itthica mag10 and pulled down on the beast that was about to assault me. Now fortunately I was sensible enough to revisit all of the details of the situation before I fired. This huge turtle was putting one huge toenail in front of the other and it almost made it to my kneecap when I realized that I had my magnification turned up to the point I almost didn’t realize it was actually a seed tick. Thank you Zoomies for your amazing product! Need to work on some quality aspects, I had to buy a spare pair to take on my skunk ape hunting trip because a screw fell out of my originals.

Considering the product, I wasn't hopeful. I was expecting an unusable cheap toy. However, since there was nothing like it, I thought I'd give it a try. The one I have advertised 400% magnification instead of 300%. I found that the optical quality was as good as what I would expect from a cheap set of opera glasses, but they don't stick out as far as opera glasses and seems fairly comfortable to wear. Others have asked if you can use it over glasses, but (like binoculars), the zoomies can be used without your glasses since the magnification corrects for whatever prescription you have on your glasses. So, it isn't the greatest, but quite usable.
